Settings

Keep playing animated wallpaper when other applications are focused:

Keep running will track when you interact with other applications and stop if you are interacted with another interaction. For example, when you click on google chrome, the animated wallpaper will stop!

Keep playing animated wallpaper when other applications are maximized:

Keep running will track maximized windows and only play your animated wallpaper when no other application is maximized. For example, if google chrome is in fullscreen, the animated wallpaper won't move.

Framerate:

The framerate dictates how fast frames of your video are painted, think of it like a speedometer where the higher framerate will adjust in a fastser video. For best performance, it is recommended a frame rate of around 16-60 frames per second.
